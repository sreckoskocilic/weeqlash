HOW TO PLAY

Gameplay

Each turn, move one of your pegs to an adjacent tile. The tile's category determines your trivia question.

  • Answer correctly to move your peg.
  • Answer wrong and your peg stays put.

Ranks

Each peg has a rank: Kmet → Vojnik → Vitez. Rank up by answering questions correctly on the same peg (threshold: 3 on small boards, 5 on large).

Combat

Move onto a tile occupied by an enemy peg to attack. You get two questions.

  • Answer Q1 correctly to win the combat.
  • You also win if your peg has a higher rank, even if you miss Q2.
  • Answer Q2 correctly to rank up your attacking peg.
  • The defeated peg is eliminated.

Flag Tiles

Move onto a flag tile to attempt to capture it. Answer 3 questions correctly to claim the flag. Capture all flags to win!

Keyboard Controls

  • Arrow keys — navigate the board or cycle answer options
  • A / B / C / D — pick an answer option
  • Enter — select peg/tile or confirm answer
