What we collect

When you play Weeqlash, we collect only what is needed to run the game:

  • Player name — entered by you, used only for your current game session. Never stored on our servers after the session ends.
  • Leaderboard entries — if you choose to submit a score, your chosen display name and score are stored in the public leaderboard.

What we do NOT collect

  • Email addresses, passwords, or account information
  • IP addresses or device identifiers beyond what is technically required for a WebSocket connection
  • Behavioural tracking or analytics data
  • Any data from minors knowingly

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. Leaderboard scores are public by design — do not use your real name if you prefer anonymity.

Data retention

Game session data is discarded when the session ends. Leaderboard entries are kept until reset.

HOW TO PLAY — QLASHIQUE

1v1 trivia duel. Both players start with 30 HP and take turns. To start: one player leaves the code field blank and clicks on green QLASHIQUE button to create a room, then shares the 5-letter code. The other enters the code and clicks the same button to join.

When it's your turn, click ⚔ ATTACK to start the timer. Questions appear one by one — correct: +1, wrong: −1. Keep answering until the timer runs out or you click END TURN at any point — even with score 0, in which case the turn ends with no effect. Timer starts at 5 s and grows +3 s each turn (max 25 s).

Your final score determines what happens:

  • Negative — you deal that much damage to yourself.
  • 0 — nothing happens.
  • 1 — attacks the opponent for 1 HP.
  • 2 or more — choose: attack the opponent for full score damage, or heal yourself for +2 HP.
  • +10 in one turn with no wrong answers — instant win.

The 💊 HEAL button is available during your turn (disabled if score < 2). Clicking it immediately heals +2 HP and ends your turn.
