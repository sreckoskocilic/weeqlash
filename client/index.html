HOW TO PLAY

Gameplay

Each turn you have 3 moves to spend freely across any of your pegs.

  • Each move attempt costs 1 token (whether you answer correctly or not).
  • Answer correctly to move your peg to the target tile.
  • Answer wrong and your peg stays put, but the token is spent.
  • When all 3 tokens are spent, your turn ends.

HP System

Each peg starts with 3 HP. HP never increases during the game.

Combat

Move onto a tile occupied by an enemy peg to attack. You get up to 3 questions.

  • Each correct answer deals 1 HP damage to the defender.
  • First miss ends the fight — your peg stays put, defender keeps remaining HP.
  • Reduce the defender to 0 HP to eliminate them and move into their tile.
  • Combat always ends your turn, consuming all remaining move tokens.

Flag Tiles

Move onto a flag tile to attempt to capture it. Answer 3 questions correctly to claim the flag. Capture all flags to win!

Keyboard Controls

  • Arrow keys — navigate the board or cycle answer options
  • A / B / C / D — pick an answer option
  • Enter — select peg/tile or confirm answer
